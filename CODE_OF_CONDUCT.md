# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in Project Thorondor a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community
- Discussing engineering disagreements with evidence and respect

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing another person's private information without explicit permission
- Deliberately unsafe advice, falsified test results, or concealed safety hazards
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Engineering and Safety Conduct

Because Thorondor involves rotating machinery, elevated structures, stored mechanical energy, electrical systems, and pond installations, contributors must clearly identify known hazards, assumptions, untested claims, and limitations.

Contributors must not present an experimental design as proven safe or production-ready without appropriate evidence.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, CAD, issues, and other contributions that are inconsistent with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and when an individual officially represents the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, unsafe, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to Sillmiron Studios LLC through the contact method listed in the repository profile or project documentation.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will use their judgment to determine appropriate consequences, which may include a private correction, warning, temporary restriction, or permanent removal from project spaces.
